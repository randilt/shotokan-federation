Shotokan Sri Lanka

Welcome To Shotokan Sri Lanka

Welcome to the official Shotokan Karate Federation Sri Lanka, established in 2001 as the authoritative body dedicated to the promotion and development of traditional Shotokan Karate in the country. As the pinnacle of Shotokan excellence in Sri Lanka, our federation plays a pivotal role in advancing the legacy of this martial art.
At the core of our organization is a profound commitment to upholding the values of Shotokan Karate—discipline, respect, and excellence. We serve as the unifying force for practitioners across the nation, providing a platform for comprehensive training and representing Sri Lanka in international competitions.
As the recognized authority, the Shotokan Karate Federation Sri Lanka goes beyond the physical aspects of martial arts. We are custodians of tradition, integrating modern training methodologies to ensure the continued growth and relevance of Shotokan Karate in the 21st century.

Values of Shotokan Federation Sri Lanka

Our Vision

To be the foremost authority and promoter of traditional Shotokan Karate in Sri Lanka, fostering a community of skilled practitioners united by a commitment to discipline, respect, and excellence.

Our Mission

Dedicated to advancing the art of Shotokan Karate, our mission is to promote and preserve its rich heritage, provide comprehensive training for practitioners, and build a supportive community that embodies the principles of Shotokan Karate.
