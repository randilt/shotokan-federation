Shotokan National Karate Championship 2023

Shotokan National Karate Championship recently took center stage at Colombo's Sugathadasa Indoor Stadium, coinciding with the 22nd anniversary celebration of the Shotokan Karate Federation Sri Lanka (SKF). This event brought together participants from across the country for a friendly showcase of karate skills, emphasizing camaraderie and sportsmanship. The dynamic atmosphere at Sugathadasa Indoor Stadium encapsulated the SKF's commitment to promoting Shotokan Karate's values of discipline, respect, and excellence.

With dedicated coaches, passionate spectators, and enthusiastic participants, the championship symbolized the SKF's two-decade journey in fostering martial arts excellence and building a strong Shotokan community. The event was not just a display of skills but a reflection of the SKF's lasting impact on the martial arts scene in Sri Lanka.
